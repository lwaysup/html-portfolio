My Best Movies of All Time

List of my three best movies

Life Is Beautiful

I love that it plays with your emotions. It makes you cry and laugh at the same time.

In The Mood For Love

A stunningly beautiful and romantic movie where time elapses through the dresses of the female protagonist.

L'Ete Meurtrier

Impeccable acting by French actress Isabelle Adjani. The story of a sultry and calculating young woman out to avenge a defining family trauma.
